Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common questions people ask about me and my work.

Who are you?

I am Dineo Thothe, a Business Intelligence and Data Analytics student at Botswana Accountancy College (BAC) in Francistown. I am also a passionate photographer and aspiring data professional.

What are you studying?

I am studying Business Intelligence and Data Analytics (BIDA) at BAC. The programme covers areas such as data analysis, Power BI, database design, business reporting, and web development.

What photography services do you offer?

I offer portrait, event, product, and landscape photography. I also provide basic photo editing and post-processing. Visit my Services page or Gallery for examples of my work.

What tools do you use for data analysis?

I primarily use Microsoft Power BI, Microsoft Excel, and Microsoft Access. I am comfortable with data cleaning, visualisation, DAX formulas, and relational database design.

How do I book a photography session?

Simply fill in the form on my Contact page or reach me directly by email. I will get back to you within 24 hours to discuss your requirements.

Are you available for freelance data projects?

Yes! I am available for freelance data analysis and dashboard projects. Whether you need an Excel report or a Power BI dashboard, I am happy to help.

Do you do web development?

I build clean, responsive websites using HTML and CSS as part of my studies. I am currently at an entry level but growing fast. Contact me if you are interested.
